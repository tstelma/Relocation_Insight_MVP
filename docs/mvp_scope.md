# MVP Scope

This MVP compares 28 European countries using five indicators:

1. `inflation_pressure`
2. `housing_pressure`
3. `poverty_pressure`
4. `income_capacity`
5. `net_earnings_capacity`

Direction logic:

- Lower is better for `inflation_pressure`, `housing_pressure`, and `poverty_pressure`.
- Higher is better for `income_capacity` and `net_earnings_capacity`.

The MVP includes:

- Country-level pressure, income capacity, and working-person net earnings signals.
- Key Signals and Key Risk Driver based only on pressure indicators.
- Income and earnings capacity section using PPS.
- Top 5 by Indicator rankings, including net earnings capacity.
- Current-value Country Comparison.
- Historical Trends and Cross-country Historical Trend Comparison.
- Indicator Glossary, methodology notes, and selected-country CSV export.

The MVP does not claim to be a full relocation recommendation engine.

Limitations:

- `net_earnings_capacity` is scenario-based: single person, no children, earning 100% of average earnings.
- It is a directional working-person earnings signal, not a model of all workers, households, professions, or tax situations.
- The MVP does not include city-level data, job markets, taxes/benefits beyond the selected net earnings series, healthcare, language, lifestyle fit, immigration rules, or personal preferences.
